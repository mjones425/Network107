Welcome to Network107

"The all-in-one HBCU x NFL experience!"


The name "Network107" aligns with how the sports business industry can network with students
from all 107 HBCUS across the country to provide opportunities for positions of interest.

Mission Statement

Our goal is to create a NFL portal for HBCU students, alumni, and supporting professional groups
to engage in receiving knowledge of interested careers, opportunities and events hosted
by the NFL with an objective to provide an all-in-one experience for engagement and recruitment.
